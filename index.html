Background

I am an Applied Mathematics Master’s student at Brigham Young University, specializing in the intersection of Deep Learning, Bayesian Inference, and Physiological Sensing. My work focuses on leveraging GPT-based architectures to transform how we understand cardiac health through PPG data.

As a researcher and TA for the ACME program, I bridge the gap between complex mathematical theory and scalable computational implementation.
