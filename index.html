About Me

I am a researcher and graduate student at BYU focused on the intersection of Cardiac Health Sensing and Generative AI. My work applies Bayesian inference and GPT model architectures to physiological signals.

Beyond research, I am passionate about math education and mentorship, having served as a Teaching Assistant for the ACME Senior lab courses for several years.
