Research & Innovation

Physio Foundation Model (GPT-PPG)

Currently developing a transformer-based model in PyTorch to predict heart rate and blood pressure while generating ECG signals from raw PPG data. This involves training on BYU’s supercomputer cluster using Slurm for job management.

Bayesian Inference & Spectrometer Data

Developed machine learning models to solve for Beer-Lambert Model coefficients. This work resulted in a U.S. Provisional Patent for estimating blood analytes from spectrometer signals.
